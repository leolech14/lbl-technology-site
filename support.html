Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our AI services

How does ProfilePro work?

ProfilePro uses advanced AI technology to transform your casual photos into professional headshots. Simply upload a photo, choose your preferred style, and our AI generates professional-quality results with 99.8% identity preservation in seconds.

What photo formats are supported?

We support common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. For best results, upload high-quality photos with good lighting where your face is clearly visible. Images should be at least 512x512 pixels.

How secure is my data?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We use enterprise-grade encryption, automatically delete processed images within 30 days, and comply with LGPD, GDPR, and CCPA regulations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

While our AI achieves 99.8% accuracy, we understand that results may vary. Contact our support team if you're not satisfied with your generated photos. We'll work with you to improve results or, in exceptional cases, provide account credits.

Do you offer enterprise solutions?

Yes! We provide custom AI solutions for enterprise clients, including bulk processing, API integration, custom model training, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team at sales@lbl.technology for enterprise pricing and features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Pix (for Brazilian customers), bank transfers, and other local payment methods. All transactions are processed securely through certified payment providers.
